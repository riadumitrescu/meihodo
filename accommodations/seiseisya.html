Seiseisya

Room Overview & Layout

Seiseisya embodies traditional Japanese elegance with carefully curated modern amenities. The space features tatami flooring, sliding shoji screens, and a private garden view that creates a serene atmosphere for contemplation and rest.

The layout includes a main living area with traditional low table, separate sleeping quarters with futon bedding, and a private tea corner for quiet moments throughout your stay.

Bath

Private hinoki cypress bath with mountain spring water, offering a traditional Japanese bathing experience. The bathroom also includes modern shower facilities and premium organic amenities.

Evening bath hours: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Morning bath hours: 6:00 AM - 9:00 AM

Check-in / Check-out
