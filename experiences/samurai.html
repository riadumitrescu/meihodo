Samurai Experience

Overview

Our signature Samurai Experience offers a comprehensive immersion into the world of the warrior class through authentic training, philosophical study, and cultural practice. This multi-day program combines physical disciplines with spiritual development, following the traditional path of bushido.

Led by experienced masters who have dedicated their lives to preserving samurai traditions, participants will engage in swordsmanship, meditation, calligraphy, and the study of classical texts that shaped the samurai mindset for centuries.

Course Flow

  1. Morning Meditation (Zazen) - Begin each day with seated meditation to cultivate focus and inner calm
  2. Physical Conditioning - Traditional exercises to build strength, flexibility, and endurance
  3. Swordsmanship Training - Learn proper stance, grip, and basic cutting techniques with wooden swords
  4. Bushido Philosophy - Study the way of the warrior through classical texts and discussion
  5. Tea Ceremony - Practice mindfulness and precision through the ritual of tea preparation
  6. Calligraphy (Shodō) - Express warrior virtues through traditional brush writing
  7. Evening Reflection - Journal writing and group discussion on the day's lessons

What to Bring / Attire

  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing for movement (traditional gi provided if needed)
  • Indoor training shoes or barefoot as preferred
  • Personal water bottle and towel
  • Journal and writing materials for reflection
  • Open mind and respectful attitude
  • Any personal meditation cushion (optional)

Notes / Safety

  • All training uses wooden practice swords (bokken) - no sharp blades
  • Participants must be 16 years or older
  • Basic physical fitness required - consult physician if health concerns
  • Respectful behavior and traditional etiquette expected at all times
  • Photography permitted in designated areas only
  • No alcohol or tobacco during training periods
  • Weather-dependent outdoor activities may be modified
