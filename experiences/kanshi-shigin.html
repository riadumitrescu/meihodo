Kanshi Shigin (Poetry Recital)

Poetry recital/chanting experience exploring cadence and meaning.

About This Experience

Kanshi Shigin is the traditional art of chanting Chinese classical poetry in Japanese pronunciation. This vocal expression combines literary appreciation with rhythmic intonation, creating a unique cultural experience that bridges literature and music.
