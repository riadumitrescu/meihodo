Photography Areas & Gallery

Why not take photos in our vast Japanese cultural facilities?

At Meihodo, we offer facility rentals as a place for various photography uses, mainly centered around cosplay. Our staff includes former cosplayers and former studio photographers. We provide personalized consultation and guidance for each customer's various requests and needs, so you can use our facilities with peace of mind.

SPACE

Within our vast grounds, various buildings suitable for photography are scattered throughout.

Martial arts dojo, archery range, taiko plaza, tea room, jujutsu hall, waterfall area, drum tower, meditation hall, forest path, and kiln house. Of course, outdoor photography is possible, and indoor photography is also available, making it possible to create photography that differs from regular location shoots and studio photography.

Note: Changing rooms are available. The rooms we prepare may vary depending on the number of people and the reservation status of experiences.

Facility Use Guidelines

  • First-time users must present identification (driver's license, insurance card, student ID, etc.)
  • Usage fees must be paid in advance at the front desk upon arrival
  • Facility entry is possible 15 minutes before the start time
  • Facility usage time includes changing time and is not the photography end time, so please note
  • If you exceed the facility usage time, an additional ¥1,500 per person will be charged for exit, so please note
  • Photography usage is limited to one group per day
  • Based on cancellation policy, fees may be charged for last-minute cancellations. Please understand.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring for my photography session?

Please bring your photography equipment, appropriate clothing, and identification. We provide changing rooms and basic facilities.

Can I bring props or costumes?

Yes, you can bring props and costumes. We especially welcome cosplay photography and can provide guidance on the best locations.

Is there a limit on the number of people?

We can accommodate various group sizes, but please contact us in advance for groups larger than 10 people.
