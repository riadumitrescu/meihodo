Authentic Samurai Experience and Japanese Culture in Aso

At Meihodo, surrounded by the natural beauty of Aso, explore martial arts such as Kyūdō, Karate, Kendō, and Iaidō, along with traditional culture including Taiko drumming, tea ceremony, bonseki, ikebana, seated meditation, and waterfall training. You can also try the "Samurai Experience Course" wearing martial arts attire, popular for creating memories during family trips or for couples.
